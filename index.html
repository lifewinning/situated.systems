Situated Systems

Situated Systems is an experimental collaborative site-specific research project. It explores the industrial and military history of San Francisco, and its role in shaping the region’s contemporary technology industries’ culture and products. Digital fabrication and additive manufacturing tools are used to transform the cultural, political, and spatial elements of the research into physical outputs, allowing us to share both our process and outcomes via objects, documentation, and events. Situated Systems is supported by Autodesk as the inaugural project of the Experimental Research Lab at the Pier 9 facility.
